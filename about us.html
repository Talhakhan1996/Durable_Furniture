About Us

 


    The term “DURABLE” stems from an economic concept of a good that has utility over an   extended period of time.

       "DURABILITY IS AN ASSURANCE THAT A PURCHASE WILL LAST".

    Founded in 2005, The product needed to be exceptionally durable and easy to clean without   looking institutional. Durable Furniture's started building furniture specific for this industry and   also utilizing Amish furniture builders for exceptional quality and strength. Not yet satisfied,   Durable Furniture's began using a custom finish shop and upholstery for the healthcare industry to   provide the ultimate products. We believe a unique and special industry requires a specific   solution for home furnishings.

    We are confident you will be amazed by the difference our products make....
